SEAHORSE SERENITY FACTS

Name

The name “Seahorse Serenity” simply comes from the peacefulness we’ve found from the worship of these wonderful beings.

What is the Seahorse Serenity Church?

The SSC is a group of devout believers of the seahorse faith. Not just a building, but a mindset and a lifestyle. Here, we provide resources to worship and a community of loving seahorse believers.

Vision

Our mission is to teach the public of our faith, and to spread awareness of the importance of seahorses. We strive to grow our community and funds for our teachings.

History

Our founder, Sheldon H. Olson, was a man fascinated with seahorsekind. Their culture, personality, and caring society. Since he was a child, he was captivated. He devoted his life to researching and getting to know seahorses. The beauty truly touched his soul. Wanting to share this with others, he began a club of other seahorse connoisseurs. From there, the word only spread further. With a small GoFundMe donation of 100,000,000 million dollars, Olson set off to spread his word even further. Now, the Seahorse Serenity Church has over 50 locations in every major ocean.

Services

We provide a multitude of services. Prayers, funerals, baptisms, exorcisms, and children’s activities to name a few. We are currently in the process of creating a children’s educational show, which will be available on DVD.

Location

There are over 50 locations in every major ocean, and we encourage you to research if there are any near you. We plan to attempt to build in lakes, too. This may not happen until 2050 or later.

People (and Horses)

Our preachers work their hardest to pass on the word of Sheldon Olson. If you wish to talk to one, please head over to your local church and visit in person.

Both males and females also change colour during their courtship display.

Other

Please consider our faith. We have never received a complaint from any of our members. We are a caring, accepting family who would love to have you.
